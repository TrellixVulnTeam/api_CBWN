The Lok Sabha is elected for a term of five years. Its life can be extended for one year at a time during a national emergency. It can be dissolved earlier than its term by the President on the advice of the Prime Minister.
It can be voted out of power by a debate and vote on a no-confidence motion. During the 13th Lok Sabha, Bhartiya Janata Party lost a no-confidence motion by one vote and had to resign.
